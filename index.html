Getting There

Travel to Levi

Multiple ways to reach Finnish Lapland. Choose what works for you.

🚄 Train from Helsinki

Overnight sleeper train Helsinki → Kolari (nearest station to Levi). ~12 hours. Bus/taxi connection from Kolari to Levi (~40 min). Book early on VR.fi for best prices.

✈ Fly to Kittilä

Direct flights from Helsinki to Kittilä Airport (KTT), ~1.5 hours. Levi is 15 minutes from the airport. Finnair and Norwegian operate this route.

🚗 Drive / Car Rental

~1,100 km from Helsinki. A road trip through Lapland. Consider splitting the drive over two days. A car is also handy for local trips once in Levi.

🌎 From Abroad

Fly into Helsinki (HEL) and connect to Kittilä or take the night train. Some connecting flights via Stockholm or Tromsø may also work.
